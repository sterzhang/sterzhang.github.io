About

Hi! I’m Jianshu Zhang (张鉴殊), a first-year CS Ph.D. student at Northwestern University. I am passionate about building intelligent systems that understand and act in the physical world through multimodal reasoning. I love collaborating on ambitious ideas—feel free to reach out if you’d like to chat.

Research Highlights

I am broadly interested in multimodal intelligence for embodied and agentic systems.

  • Multimodal learning that grounds language with perception and action.
  • Agentic AI for reliable, tool-augmented reasoning.
  • Spatial understanding of 3D environments and complex scenes.
